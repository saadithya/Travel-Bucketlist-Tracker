Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 25, 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using Travel Bucketlist Tracker — a Chrome extension that helps users track countries and Indian states they’ve visited through interactive maps. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. All your travel data (such as visited countries or states) is stored locally on your device using Chrome’s storage API.

How Your Data is Used

  • Your travel data is used solely to display your personal progress on the maps.
  • No data leaves your device or is shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

This extension uses Leaflet and OpenStreetMap tiles to render maps. These map tiles are requested securely over HTTPS from their respective servers. No personal data is sent in these requests.

Permissions Explained

  • storage – to save your visited locations locally.
  • tabs – to allow popup actions with the current tab (no browsing history or content is read).

Data Security

All local data is stored securely in your browser and is not accessible to other websites or extensions. You can clear your data anytime from the extension’s settings or by clearing Chrome’s local storage.

Children’s Privacy

Travel Bucketlist Tracker does not target or knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
