Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Travel Bucketlist Tracker — a Chrome extension that helps users visualize their travels by marking visited countries and Indian states on interactive maps. Your privacy is important to us. This policy outlines how your data is handled when using the extension.

2. Information We Collect

The extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. All travel data (such as visited countries or states) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage API.

3. How Your Data Is Used

  • Your marked locations are stored locally and used only to show progress on your personal map.
  • No data leaves your device or is shared with any server.
  • We do not use analytics, cookies, or tracking technologies.

4. Permissions Explained

4.1 storage

Used to save and retrieve your travel progress (visited locations) locally on your device.

4.2 tabs

Used only to support popup interactions with your current browser tab. The extension does not access your browsing history or web content.

5. Third-Party Services

Travel Bucketlist Tracker uses Leaflet and OpenStreetMap for displaying map data. Map tiles are fetched securely over HTTPS directly from their servers. No personal data is shared or transmitted in these requests.

6. Data Security

All your data is securely stored within Chrome’s local storage system. This data is sandboxed to the extension and not accessible to other websites or extensions. You may clear your travel data anytime through the extension’s settings or by clearing your browser’s local storage.

7. Children’s Privacy

Travel Bucketlist Tracker does not target or knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any modifications will be reflected here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced through the extension’s update notes or official GitHub page.
