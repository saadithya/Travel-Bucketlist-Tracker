Travel Bucketlist Tracker

Visualize your travels. Track every country and Indian state you’ve explored.

Install from Chrome Web Store

About the Extension

Travel Bucketlist Tracker lets you easily mark countries and Indian states you've visited on interactive world and India maps. Your data is stored locally in your browser — private, simple, and accessible anytime.

Features

  • ✅ Mark visited countries and Indian states
  • 🗺️ View your travel progress on dynamic maps
  • 🔒 Data stays private — saved locally in your browser
  • ⚡ Clean, fast, and user-friendly design
  • 🌍 Built using Leaflet and OpenStreetMap

Why You'll Love It

Whether you're a traveler, explorer, or dreamer, this extension helps you see your journey so far and inspires your next destination.
